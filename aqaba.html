Aqaba



Aqaba was definetly a very memorable and important place to many, specifically during lockdowns, and times during COVID. After the travel ban, my family and I spent weeks on end in Aqaba.

This picture was taken on my last day there. The sunset was so pretty on top of the mountains. I will most definelty never forget the memories I have had in Aqaba, and hopefully there are more to come.
